Research Participation Consent

Study:

What you will do: Record your dreams in this journal over the course of your participation. After writing each dream, you will answer a brief questionnaire (8-14 questions) about your experience in the dream. You may also optionally classify the dream using a guided walkthrough.

Time required: Each dream entry takes approximately 5-10 minutes. You are asked to record dreams as soon as possible after waking.

Confidentiality: Your dream texts and responses are stored securely and identified only by a participant code. Your name is not linked to your data. Only authorized researchers have access to the data.

Voluntary participation: Your participation is voluntary. You may stop at any time without penalty. You may skip any dream you prefer not to record.

Risks: Recording dreams may occasionally bring up uncomfortable memories or emotions. If you experience distress, please contact your study coordinator or therapist.

Benefits: You will be able to view your own dream patterns over time. Your data contributes to research on dream structure and therapeutic change.
